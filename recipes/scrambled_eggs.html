Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs are high in protein and very nutritious. Here is a simple scrambled eggs recipe for your files.


Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp heavy cream
  • 1/2 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper

Directions:

  1. Break 3 eggs into a bowl and scramble them with heavy cream.
  2. Heat up frying pan and add butter.
  3. When the frying pan is hot, pour in the egg mixture and add salt and pepper.
  4. Scramble the eggs until firm
  5. Plate them and add some nutritional yeast.
