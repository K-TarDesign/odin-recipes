Fresh Ground Coffee

Using a French press is a great approach for making fresh ground coffee. While it may require a little more attention than other methods to make a good good cup of coffee, you also have more control over the end result. Here is a recipe for making a cup of fresh ground coffee using a French press.

Ingredients:

  • 15 grams of ground coffee per 8 oz water
  • Boiling water

Directions:

  1. Grind the coffee beans.
  2. Put the ground coffee beans into a French press.
  3. Add boiling water and let sit for 3 minutes, then stir gently.
  4. Let sit for another 3-5 minutes, and pour into a cup.
